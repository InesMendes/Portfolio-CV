The Creative life

Apart from being a QA tester, I have had a love for design and illustration since I was young. In 2013 I started volunteering through the UN online program, and have since worked with many organizations all over the world. During this time, I developed my Graphic Design skills beyond what I could imagine. I have designed brochures, t-shirts, logos, websites, vouchers, ... Through this, I have been invited for several professional projects outside volunteering, which has been an invaluable experience.
